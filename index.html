About Me

Hi, I'm Juri, and this is where I put my crap and hang out with a few honorable netizens.

Heads up: Desktop view is the recommended experience, but the site is optimized for mobile. It's hosted on GitHub Pages, and I built it on a 1920x1080 laptop. Hand-coded—if you don’t count the formatting. Lately, I’ve been learning how to code and program, so I see this place as both an archive and a homage to that. Yeah, it looks straight out of the '90s, but that’s not about being stuck in the past—I just like the simplicity, and it’s easier to maintain. For chat, I use Cbox now. I used to run my own backend, but I got tired of maintaining it.

My interests mostly revolve around tech, music, visual novels, self-isolation, and gothic lolita punk J-fashion & subculture. I also have a random Charmmy Kitty obsession. My favorite artist of all time is Velvet Acid Christ. Anyway, not sure what else to add—happy browsing!
