Juri's Stuff

I'm Juri, based in California. I'm into old tech, audio production, visual novels, and gothic lolita punk J-fashion. Mental and psychiatric health are important to me — I'm neurodivergent, and navigating the world sometimes feels a little different. I try to move at my own pace, taking one day at a time and giving myself room to grow. I enjoy coding, hiking, making music, DJing, baking, and journaling. I usually keep to myself, but I'm always open to conversation if you ever feel like reaching out. Work-wise, I've done a bit of everything from food service and hospitality to computer repair.
