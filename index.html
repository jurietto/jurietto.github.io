⛧ ABOUT 🩷

So basically this site exists because traditional social media makes my brain feel weird and I need somewhere I can be myself. I mainly use this space to mess around with basic web design - HTML, CSS, JavaScript, the usual suspects. Nothing fancy just me learning stuff and breaking things accidentally... sometimes I fix them sometimes I don't! It's whatever. Thoughts, feelings, random confusion, projects I start and forget about, things I'm supposedly working on... it all lives in in this little hermit hideout. Sometimes my online friends hang out here when Discord gets boring!!! We just pretend we're celebrities and start rambling to ourseleves in the chatbox like lost souls except with WIFI. Honestly the whole thing is a bit messy or chaotic a bit maybe and the design will probably change because I can't make creative decisions to save my life, but that's part of the fun I guess.
