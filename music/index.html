⛧ MUSIC

This is where I dump thoughts about music shit that has consumed my brain and probably saved it from complete meltdown more times than I can count.

Music is the best medicine I've found for dealing with just about everything. Whether I'm stressed out about something, pissed off at the state of the world, or just trying to get through another day of scrolling through manufactured BS on my phone - music pulls me back to something that feels transformative. It saves lives, countless every day, in all types of ways like some strange magic that's also science but feels like pure electricity rewiring your brain.

I'm drawn to electronic music, mostly underground stuff and mainstream too - no hate. I like Electro Industrial, EBM, Techno, Industrial Dance, Breakcore, Deconstructed Club, Hyperpop, Hardstyle, UK Happy Hardcore, plus random oldies and lost hits from the 70's that somehow still hit harder than most modern shit.
