Privacy Policy

Data Collection

This site collects minimal data. When you post or comment, we store:

  • Username (optional, defaults to "Anonymous")
  • Post/Comment text (what you write)
  • Images (if you upload attachments)
  • Timestamp (when you posted)
  • Hashtags (if you use them)

We do NOT collect:

  • IP addresses
  • Cookies
  • Browser/device information
  • Location data
  • Any tracking pixels or analytics

Data Storage

All posts and comments are stored in Google Firebase Firestore, a cloud database service. Data is publicly readable—anyone can see your posts and comments. We do not use any analytics platforms.

External Services

YouTube videos: We use privacy-enhanced YouTube embeds. Videos only load when you click to play them. YouTube does not track your presence on this site until you click a video.

Links: We strip tracking parameters (utm_*, fbclid, gclid) from user-shared links. Links open in a new tab with no referrer information.

Security

This site uses:

  • Content Security Policy to prevent script injection
  • No-referrer policy to prevent referrer leaks
  • Permissions Policy to disable unnecessary browser features
  • Input validation to prevent XSS attacks
  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit (via GitHub Pages)

Your Rights

Since this is a small personal project on GitHub:

  • Posts and comments are public by default
  • We do not sell or share your data
  • To delete your content, contact the site administrator
  • We have no ads, no sponsorships, and no third-party tracking

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. Changes will be posted here. Last updated: January 31, 2026

Questions?

This is a privacy-first site built for net friends. If you have concerns about privacy or security, feel free to ask.
