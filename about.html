DJing & Music

I've been producing music since 2017 and DJing since 2021. My music mainly falls into electronic dance genres—people often describe it as happy hardcore or hard dance. You can check out my tracks on SoundCloud.

I listen to all kinds of music, but my favorite genres are EBM, electro-industrial, trance, hardstyle, and happy hardcore. There’s something to appreciate about all music.

My favorite artist is Velvet Acid Christ. Other favorites include Goreshit, Lords of Acid, Eisenfunk, DJ Billybool, God Module, and Ladytron.
