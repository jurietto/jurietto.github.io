DJing & Music 

I've been producing music since 2017 and DJing since 2021. My music mainly falls into electronic dance genres—people often describe it as sounding like happy hardcore or hard dance. You can check out my music on SoundCloud and decide for yourself!

I listen to all kinds of music, but my favorite genres are probably EBM, electro-industrial, trance, hardstyle, and happy hardcore. There’s something to appreciate about all music.

My favorite artist is Velvet Acid Christ. Other favorites include Goreshit, Lords of Acid, Eisenfunk, DJ Billybool, God Module, and Ladytron.

Baking 

Baking rocks! I used to be an overnight baker, and during that time, I read a lot about it and maintained an entire kitchen. In my free time, I like reading about pastries from around the world.

Visual Novels 

My favorite visual novel is Monochrome, and my first was Cafe Little Wish. I also collect old PS2 and PSX NTSC-J titles. I archive playthroughs and am learning how to translate them.
